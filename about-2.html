About

SilviLaser 2027 will bring together experts from different fields to discuss the newest in cutting edge technology and science from the laser scanning and forest communities. Themes for this year’s conference will cover multiple aspects of laser scanning in a forestry context; ranging from terrestrial, mobile, UAV, airborne and space borne lidar instruments.

This year’s event will be held in Warsaw, Poland and will include presentations from keynote speakers, parallel sessions, workshops and information sessions.

SilviLaser is a well-attended, biannual event that has been occurring for over 20 years.
